ABOUT G&G SANDWICHES

G&G Sandwiches is dishing up the best panini in the Palm Springs area. They offer both classic and unique panini options that are sure to please your palate.

Known for their Philly Cheesesteak, G&G Sandwiches offers plenty of items for every palate to try. The panini are toasted to perfection, the menu features many sandwiches and salads, as well as more popular dishes like Lupe’s Chicken Special and the Chef Salad. Don’t wait to try out their new menu item “Spicy Philly Cheesesteak”. It is a must try when visiting Palm Springs.

“In this world, there are sandwiches we eat and then there are sandwiches we get excited about eating. The latter of those two are almost always grilled — like panini.”

-Julie Thomson of The Huffington Post

Opening hours: Everyday from 6am to 5pm.

Pick-up Location: Desert Hot Springs
